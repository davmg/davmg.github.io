Mimic

Salespeople training

Vehicle training mobile app for automobile salespeople to better close deals. Mimic encourages users to “imitate” or reproduce the information and skills presented in the platform.

© Squareball Studios, 2019

Personal Highlight

Managed to interact with multiple stakeholders and to gather requirements from a complex context.

I designed a complete UI library that worked to maintain consistency across teams.

What I did

Requirement analysis, Interaction design, Prototyping, UI design & animation

Tools

Interviews, Interaction Flow, Use case scenarios, Figma.

Background

Salespeople in automotive industry need to keep track of a large amount of information and to better close deals.

Understanding

The client was a major automotive dealership chain, who had identified that information is not effectively distributed to salespeople.

They had gathered relevant content about sales skills and cars and needed a way to reach the sales force.

Early-stage process included experience understanding and mapping of all the parties involved.

Ideation

Low-fi prototypes were made in order to present to stakeholders.

It involves all the parts that take part in the experience and served as starting point to identify opportunities.

Interface Design

A complete hi-fidelity prototype was delivered for development.

Through a mobile app and a web content management system, the company would be able to distribute learning content to their representatives and improve sales rates.
