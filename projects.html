Domu

Domu is a digital and physical community for pacients staying at Fundación Valle del Lili pediatric hospitalization center. It is intended to be a tool for strengthening the psychosocial support program and the development of coping mechanisms.

What I did / Design research, experience design, prototyping, game development, user testing.

Money Sensei

Money Sensei is a personal finances advisor mobile app, which helps people to monitor their money and recommends what they may or should not do to keep their budget safe until next payday.

What I did / Interaction design, interface design, prototyping, app programming, recommendation algorithm.

Hoy es Diseño

Hoy es Diseño is the biggest design event in southern Colombia, it is entirely organized by Interactive Media Design and Industrial Design students. I made part of the 2017 first semester’s version.

What I did / Art direction, visual design.

Results / The event took place on April 27 and 28, 2017 with special lecturers coming from Europe and the Americas.

Sensturo

Artistic interactive installation in which users-spectators get immersed in a possible perception of the future. Actions and reactions around Sensturo make people experience through their senses

What I did / Interaction desgin, electronics.

Results / The installation was exhibited in La Tertulia Museum in Cali, Colombia, as part of the exhibtion “Return to the future”.

Legalapp

Mobile app that works as an assistant for academic tasks of law students. It has functionalities for making a debate, organize homeworks, store documents and a case-study simulator.

What I did / User experience, interaction design, interface design.
